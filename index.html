My Profile

I'm Joan Barduena Reyes, graduated in video game design and development in CITM, focused on the art branch of the degree, but with great knowledge in coding.

Born in Barcelona, I have always lived his urban culture and appreciate his street life. Since I was little I have been riding BMX, skating, scootering and now I'm surfing. I love to travel to discover new cultures, try new food and see the world in another perspective. I'm always motivated to learn something new, always pushing and trying my best to achieve the results I want.

I really like to code and design, that's why I started a Master's degree in Interaction & UX/UI Design.
