S-TEAM
Category: Videogame
Year: 2021
Team Size: 5

S-TEAM is a 3D Puzzle game developed in Unity where the player must swap between five woman, each one with huge knowledge on one field of STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art and Mathematics).

The game was developed during 34 hours (non-stop) at CITM Game Jam 4th Edition.

During the project we created all rock props, wood fences, boxes, tablets, amoung others in Blender.
