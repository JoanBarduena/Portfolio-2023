Joker: The Final Show
Category: Videogame
Year: 2021
Team Size: 2

Joker: The Final Show is a third-person shooter developed in Unreal Engine 4.

The game is inspired by the Joker film of 2019 and it starts just after Joker shoots Frankling Murray in the head while the show was broadcasting live.

The objective is to plant two bombs while surviving SWAT agents and reach the rooftop to escape the crime scene.

I programmed the game's Artificial Intelligence as well as all player controlls (walk, run, shoot, etc) with Unreal Blueprints.
