Command & Conquer: WarZone
Category: Videogame
Year: 2018
Team Size: 7

Command and Conquer Red Alert 2: WarZone is an isometric tower defense video game. My classmates and I had to create whatever game we wanted but with the original game assets.

C&C: WarZone main goal is to destroy the enemy townhall. To do this, the player must place barracks, defense towers, buy soldiers and upgrade those.

Apart from the game, we have also created a wiki including a GDD, TDD, art and audio bible among other things.
