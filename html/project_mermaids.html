The Mermaids' Knot
Category: Videogame
Year: 2021
Team Size: 2

The Mermaids' Knot is a small RPG video game based on the dungeon map Trilemma Adventures.

The player has to stop the mermaids in order to save the village of Magda. To do so, he needs to resolve several puzzles and explore the map.

The game was developed in RPGMakerMV in two weeks.
