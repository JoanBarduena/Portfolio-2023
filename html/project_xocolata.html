Museu Xocolata (rebranding)
Category: Website
Year: 2023

Rebranding project for the Museu de Xocolata located in Barcelona. This museum has an old website with a poor brand impact.

I created an impactful logotype that can fully respresent their brand and what they are selling and showing in their museum. Also, chosing a chocolate color palette and great images to show the power of chocolate.
